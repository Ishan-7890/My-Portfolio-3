sushila devi auditorium

Sushila Devi, a multipurpose and centrally air-conditioned auditorium with impeccable acoustics, is built to host meetings, training, performances, special lectures, small events. It has the capacity of holding 140 people, with an area of 2500 sq ft, the auditorium is the hub of SAI International School, where the students are brought in for rendezvous with eminent personalities.
